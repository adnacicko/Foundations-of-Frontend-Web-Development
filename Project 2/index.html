About

How we started

  • 2019-2023

    Our Humble Beginnings

    Nestled in the heart of the city, E T N A began its journey as a humble dream. Inspired by the fiery spirit of Mount Etna, our founders envisioned a restaurant that would erupt with flavor, warmth, and hospitality.

  • March 2024

    A Restaurant is Born

    In the bustling streets of our city, amidst the whispers of culinary dreams, a new star emerges on the gastronomic horizon. Like a phoenix rising from the flames, a restaurant is born, its name whispered with anticipation: E T N A.

  • 2024

    Our Mission

    At E T N A, our mission is more than just serving exceptional food—it's about creating memorable experiences infused with passion, authenticity, and a deep commitment to our guests and community.
